Welcome to Sudoku Hatchling

Sudoku Hatchling is a fun and engaging puzzle game that takes you on a journey from egg to owl mastery! Solve Sudoku challenges, crack daily rewards, and grow your skills across levels designed for players of all ages and abilities.

1. Enter Your Name

Before choosing a level, enter your name to personalize your experience.

2. Choose a Level

Select from the following levels:

  • Eggshell (Beginner)
  • Cracked Egg (Easy)
  • Chick (Intermediate)
  • Fledgling (Advanced)
  • Wise Owl (Expert)

3. Start the Puzzle

Each Sudoku board has 9×9 boxes divided into smaller 3×3 grids. Some boxes already have numbers filled in for you.

4. Fill the Empty Boxes

Tap on an empty box, then choose a number (1–9) from the options below. Make sure every row, column, and 3×3 grid contains each number only once.

5. Earn Hints by Hunting Eggs

Look for the Hunt the Egg icon on the board. To collect the egg, complete the entire row where the egg is located. Each egg can be exchanged for a helpful hint!

6. Complete the Puzzle

Keep going until every box is filled correctly — congratulations, you’ve cracked the challenge!
